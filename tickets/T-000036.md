## Goal
Allow one human player to choose white or black against Stockfish and interact with legal moves directly on the Play-tab board.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Play tab includes mode selection for `Stockfish vs Stockfish` and `Human vs Stockfish`, with human side selector for white/black.
- [ ] In human mode, clicking a legal source square and destination square applies a legal move on the Play board.
- [ ] After each human move, Stockfish responds automatically for the engine side and updates board state/move info.
- [ ] If human side is set to black and human mode starts, Stockfish makes the first move for white.

## Goal
Implement a configurable domino chain module with trigger support and telemetry hooks.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] Domino generator supports 50 to 200 pieces.
- [x] Spacing and material presets are adjustable at runtime.
- [x] Chain can be triggered by a falling ball or explicit trigger action.
- [x] Metrics for count, average fall time, collision events, and max velocity are emitted.
